Our Repertoire

Giselle

Working with professional artists and Choreographers

In June 2017 Ballet Bloch Canada students were privileged to dance Giselle learning both classical repertoire from the ballet and new choreography. World renowned choreographer Monica Proenca re-created part of Act 2 with contemporary-based style works to compliment the classical repertoire. Guest artists included: Evan L'Hirondelle (former Arts Umbrella student now with National Ballet School of Canada); Thys Armstrong (Coastal City Ballet); Jake Preece (Ballet West II); Isabelle Chang (Ballet West II); and Diego Ramalho (Coastal City Ballet).

A Midsummer Night's Dream

Performance opportunities with professionals

In June 2016 Ballet Bloch Canada students shared the stage with six professional dancers: Sophia Lee, Principle of Royal Winnipeg Ballet; Michael Linsmeier, Soloist of Oregon Ballet Theatre; Joshua Beamish, Artist and Choreographer MOVE the company; Mark Dennis, Ballet Kelowna; Joshua Haban, Centre Stage: On Pointe (movie); and Jake Preece, Ballet West II.

Les Sylphides

PAS DE DEUX OPPORTUNITIES FOR ADVANCED STUDENTS

In June 2015 Ballet Bloch Canada staged the classical repertoire of Les Sylphides. The Corps de Ballet consisted of 11 to 16yr old students with the advanced students performing the lead and pas de deux roles.
